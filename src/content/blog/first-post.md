# Embracing Neobrutalism

Neobrutalism is a design trend that has been gaining traction. It rejects the overly clean and flat designs that have dominated the web for the past decade, opting instead for bold colors, high contrast, and a somewhat "raw" or "unpolished" aesthetic.

## Key Elements

*   **Bold Colors:** Forget subtle pastels. Neobrutalism loves bright, clashing colors.
*   **High Contrast:** Black borders are thick and everywhere.
*   **Hard Shadows:** Drop shadows aren't blurred; they are solid blocks of color, often black, offset from the element.

It's a fantastic way to make a statement and ensure your site stands out!
